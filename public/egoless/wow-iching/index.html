Quoting the Ancient Aliens TV documentary:

“According to mythology, the I-Ching was created by Emperor Fu Xi after he was visited by an otherworldly being. It was said that one day he was on the Yellow River and a water dragon appeared to him, and that on the back of this water dragon was revealed to him the I-Ching. And therefore we can see this system as in some way having come from the stars, originally.”

The I-ching is thus a code that the ancient aliens wanted to use to communicate with us.

Mathematician Gottfried Leibniz was inspired by the I-ching to create the binary code.

The binary code acts as a sort of “Rosetta Stone” to decrypt the Wow! Signal.

Wow! Signal (ASCII) -> binary -> I-ching

source: https://www.theblackvault.com/casefiles/deciphering-wow-signal-1977-using-ascii-binary-conversion/
